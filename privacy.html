Privacy Policy

Your privacy is simple: We don't collect your personal data.

Effective Date: 31 October 2025

Our Core Principle

Cablorg is a static website. We do not have user accounts, databases, or analytics trackers. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any of your personal information. Your visit is anonymous.

What We "Collect" (And Why)

The only piece of data this website "collects" is your theme preference. We do this to improve your user experience, and it works as follows:

  • Theme Preference: When you click the Dark Mode or Light Mode toggle, your choice is saved in your web browser's localStorage.
  • What is localStorage? This is a small file stored only on your computer. It is not sent to us, it never leaves your browser, and we have no access to it.
  • Why? This file simply tells your browser "This user prefers dark mode" so that the next time you visit, the site will automatically load with the theme you chose.

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's cache or site data.

Information We Do Not Collect

To be perfectly clear, we do not collect or track:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your IP address.
  • Any browser or device information.
  • Any cookies or tracking pixels.
  • Any data on how you browse this website.

Third-Party Links (Social Media)

Our website contains links to our social media pages (YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.). These are external websites. If you click these links, you will be taken to their sites, which are governed by their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

We are not a data company, so this policy is unlikely to change in any significant way. If we do make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
