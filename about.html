Engineered by Enthusiasts

Cablorg is a new design & engineering venture backed by PCMIX Computers, one of India's most trusted open-box hardware retailers. We aren't just a brand; we're a team of hardcore PC users building the products we've always wanted.

Our Story

This started from a simple, shared frustration.

The Cable Nightmare

As hardcore PC users, we've all been there. The rats' nest of cables hanging beneath the desk. The fear of touching a single wire, knowing it could unplug your entire setup. Even after hours of "cable management" with zip ties and tape, the result is often a rigid, messy block that you can never change or upgrade.

We knew there had to be a better way. We started designing our own cable management solutions focused on one goal: to make the process effortless, minimal, and aesthetically pleasing.

The Cluttered Desk

Our second frustration was the desktop itself. A premium pair of headphones. A pro-grade controller. All fighting for space, creating a constant mess.

To solve this, we're developing a line of premium stands and organizational tools. More than just a simple "headphone stand," we're creating integrated "gadget stands" designed to elegantly hold and display your most-used peripherals in one minimal footprint.

Our Process & The Future

The R&D Phase

We are perfectionists. That's why Cablorg is currently in an intensive research and development phase. All R&D is conducted in-house at our headquarters at PCMIX Computers in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We are meticulously iterating on every design, ensuring every product is durable, functional, and beautiful.

Crafted with Precision

To rapidly prototype and produce our first batches, we are leveraging high-end 3D printing technology. Our focus is on achieving a premium, high-quality finish that rivals traditional manufacturing. This allows us to perfect our designs with precision.

After a successful launch, we plan to invest in industrial molds to bring our products to an even larger scale, while maintaining the quality we demand.

Target Launch: Mid-2026

We will not rush. We plan to launch our first product lines by mid-2026, but only once we are 100% satisfied with their design and performance. Perfection is in the details, and we're committed to getting every detail right.
